Description:
Write two paragraphs (150–200 words) that describe your aesthetic intent and what you were interested in exploring technically.
I encourage you to focus on simple ideas (dots, grids, etc.), although a crescendo that peaks with total pixel chaos is always tempting...

I wanted to test differnt effects to use with simple shapes. I stuck with circles for this assignment as I wanted to test what
I could do with the shape more than what shapes I could make. Sin and cos waves were especially helpful for making things feel like they
were pulsing.
Visually I went with a constant pulse of sunset colors. This way the color was unifying and not totally boring but I could focus on the 
shapes. My piece reminds me of sunsets and space, which aren't particularliy related I suppose but thats the vibes I was working with.
Lots of spreading and shrinking from the sunset lense, as well as the color, and then some lone light on a black background always
makes me think of space.
Technically speaking, I looked at moving objects with waves, for loops, and using the previous frame.
